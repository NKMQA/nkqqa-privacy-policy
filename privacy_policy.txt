**Privacy Policy for NKMQA Bug Capture**

Effective Date: [26/02/2026]

NKMQA Bug Capture is a Chrome extension designed to help QA testers capture bug notes and screenshots.

**Third-Party Access:**
- No third-party services or websites have access to your bug reports.
- All data remains only on your local machine.

**Security:**
